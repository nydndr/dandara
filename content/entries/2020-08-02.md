-   How to handle StatusBar on phones when developing with React Native.

    I still get mad sometimes at the breach between iOS and Android React Native...

-   How to keep keyboards out of the way when having long forms on a screen.

    (The short answer is: using `<KeyboardAvoidView/>`. The [long answer](https://medium.com/@nickyang0501/keyboardavoidingview-not-working-properly-c413c0a200d4) is:`<KeyboardAvoidView>` won't work by himself.)
